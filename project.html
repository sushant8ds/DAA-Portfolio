Project Overview

1. Problem Statement

Problem: In the modern world, the rapid advancement of technology in smart cities raises concerns about privacy, security, fraud prevention, and sustainable tech use. We need to ensure that citizens can enjoy the benefits of smart city infrastructure without compromising safety, data privacy, and environmental sustainability.

2. Business Ideas

  1. 1:Anti-Fraud Browser Extension
    • Description: Detects phishing websites, fake e-commerce platforms, and suspicious URLs, providing real-time warnings to users.
    • Revenue Model: Freemium model with premium features.
  2. 2:Smart Tracker for Personal Belongings
    • Description: Affordable GPS trackers for wallets, bags, and keys with a mobile app for real-time updates.
  3. 3:Secure Family Monitoring App
    • Description: Tracks family members' locations, secures shared devices, and provides emergency alerts.
  4. 4:Smart Bike Tracking System
    • Description: A GPS tracking system for bicycles that can notify the owner if the bike is moved or stolen.
  5. 5:Solar-Powered Smart Streetlights
    • Description: Streetlights equipped with solar panels that can adjust brightness based on time of day or traffic.
  6. 6:Community Engagement App
    • Description: An app that allows citizens to report issues, engage with local government, and stay informed about community events.
